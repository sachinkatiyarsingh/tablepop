Why we started

TablePop is an event platform changing the way people design their entertaining experiences. Our aim is to create on-demand solutions for personal, social & corporate events to give access to an effortlessly artful affair.

TablePop was founded with a simple principle: resetting entertaining to suit the modern host. Our mission goes beyond the event and into the heart of family, friends, community & fellowship. We design real events for real people.

TablePop was started as a direct result of a problem encountered by its founder: The pain of spending endless hours trying to curate and style a milestone birthday dinner. That same month she set out to fix the problem and TablePop was born.
